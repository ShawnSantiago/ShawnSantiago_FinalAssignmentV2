What is Floatti?

Imagine a world where Floats don't exist and making a website was a easy a tying your shoes. Well for those of us who aren't clinically insane that world doesn't exist, but in place of a imagary world we have Floatti! The world's first Float warrior.

Features

  • Works On All Devices

    Floatti can refresh any HTML5 web browser. OS X, iOS 6+, Windows 7+ and Android 4.4+ are all supported. Every major browser, every major OS

  • Dead Simple

    There are no browser plugins to install and no setup. Just click the "Floatit" button, then sit back and enjoy the magic. That's it.

  • Simple Pricing

    We only use Western Union, Personal Checks or Government Bribes. No other forms of curreny will be accepted.
